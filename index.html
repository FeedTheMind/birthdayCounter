Once a year you get to enjoy the greatest byproduct of birth—your birthday!

What makes a birthday extra special is when it lands on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday.

With this application, you can determine how many weekend (or weekday) birthdays you will have from now until your chosen year.

To see if you will be celebrating as a weekend warrior, select the month and day when you were born, followed by any year.
